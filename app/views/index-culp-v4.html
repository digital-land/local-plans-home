About this collection

This collection gathers together resources for local planning authorities (LPAs) to use when creating a local plan.

This page is a first step towards a new home for local plans information that will cover the end-to-end plan-making process. We’ll be adding more practical resources to help you at all stages of the plan-making process.

These resources reflect the current planning policy framework for local plans.

Upcoming changes to local plans

We intend to implement the new plan-making system as set out in the Levelling-up and Regeneration Act from summer or autumn 2025.

We’ll publish more information here about the new system as it’s available.

Select ‘Get emails about this page’ above to be notified when we add new pages.

Planning practice guidance

The Planning practice guidance (PPG) sets out more detail on how to apply planning policies.

The PPG pages most relevant to preparing a local plan are included in the sections below.

We still recommend looking at the PPG to see the full range of guidance that might be relevant to your own plan. In future, we’re aiming to restructure this guidance to be clearer about what information you need at different stages of creating a local plan.

Selecting sites for development (future system)

This is draft guidance that will apply to plans that come forward under the new plan-making system from summer or autumn 2025, once it takes effect.

If you’re preparing your local plan for submission under the current plan-making system, you do not need to have regard to it. 

In future, this guidance is intended to replace all previous guidance on site selection, including the ‘Housing and economic land availability assessment’ guidance linked in the section above.  

  • Selecting sites for development for your local plan
    • 30 November 2024
