About this collection

This collection gathers together resources that cover the practical steps involved in creating a local plan.

The resources are intended to support the legislation and national policy framework by setting out the government’s recommended approach to plan-making. They should help local planning authorities (LPAs) apply the requirements in practice, which should lead to faster, clearer and more accessible local plans.

The resources here so far are a first step. We intend to keep adding more resources over time, which will eventually cover the end-to-end process of making a local plan.

The information reflects the current policy around local plans. We'll update the resources to reflect any changes, including any changes from the 2023 consultation.
